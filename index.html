What remains of mankind lives underground, watched by its own sins.

The stars above are silent. No one knows if silence means peace, or extinction.

Ashlin, a tunneler—“rock bitch,” in her own words—endures the Siege Directive’s doctrine of endless preparation. But how do you defy extinction, when survival was the first mistake?
